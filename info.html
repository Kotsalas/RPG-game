INFO

  • XP(): You can gain xp by slaying monters! The stronger the monster the better the gain of your xp! Xp makes you deal more damage and take less to even no damage from the monsters! Make sure to spend some time to gain your experince and become an excellent fighter!
  • HEALTH(): Once your Health becomes 0, it's time for you to meet the heavens...
  • GOLD(): With gold you can buy new and stronger weapons from the store! You can also heal yourself in the pharmacy section inside the store! You can gain gold by slaying monsters and selling your old weapons! Spend your gold wisely traveler!..
  • MONSTERS: There are three different types of monsters: the Slime, the Fanged Beast and the Dragon! Each monster has different stats! Make sure to challenge them while your stats are greater!
  • WEAPONS: You beggin your journey with a stick as your first weapon. As you get stronger, kill more monsters and gain experience, you'll be able to forge some greater weapons to help you reach your destination! You can check your inventory to see your current weapon. Finally you can sell your old and useless weapons, but no matter what you should always have at least one weapon! Be aware that weapons have a slight chance to brake as you use them!
  • BEATING THE GAME: In order to beat the game you have to slay the powerfull dragon that lurkes in the nearby village! Its hideout is located inside the volcano!
  • EASTER EGG: There is an easter egg inside the game let's see if you are capable of finding it! It could either make your journey easier or even kill you, choose wisely!
